# Information for Leagues

This section contains information for _FIRST_ Tech Challenge league leaders.

## Schedule Overview

Following is an overview of the 2026–2027 season schedule:

- **September 12, 2026**: Season kickoff ([learn more](./kickoff.md))
- **October 24, 2026**: First weekend for League Meet events
- **February 13, 2027**: Last weekend for League Tournament events
- **March 12–13, 2027**: Florida Championship
- **April 28–May 1, 2027**: _FIRST_ Championship

Within these guidelines, leagues may plan their events according to venue availability and team preferences.
