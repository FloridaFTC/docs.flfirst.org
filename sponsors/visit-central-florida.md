!!! About Visit Central Florida
The Real Florida Magic is here in Central Florida, a region of 17 charming communities, including [Lakeland](https://visitcentralflorida.org/cities-towns/lakeland/) and [Winter Haven](https://visitcentralflorida.org/cities-towns/winter-haven/), that are filled with natural beauty and surprising discoveries.
From historic gems, extraordinary attractions and thrilling theme parks to epic outdoor adventures, a vacation here feels worlds away, yet is still close to Orlando.
