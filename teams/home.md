# Overview

This section contains information for current and prospective _FIRST_ Tech Challenge teams.

### Season Updates

Following is an overview of the 2026–2027 season schedule:

- **September 12, 2026**: Season kickoff ([learn more](./event-types.md#kickoff))
- **October–January**: League Meets
- **January–February**: League Tournaments
- **March 12–13, 2027**: Florida Championship
- **April 28–May 1, 2027**: _FIRST_ Championship

We're pleased to announce the creation of a new league, the **Southwest Florida League**.
Teams along the southern Gulf Coast will enjoy shorter commutes to their events this season.
More information will be communicated directly to affected coaches.
